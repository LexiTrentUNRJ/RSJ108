How to Conduct a Article Interview

Prepping Time: 20 minutes
Interview Duration: 5 minutes

Are you struggling with conducting interviews for your upcoming article? Follow these steps to make you next interview the best yet!

  • Interview Checklist:

  • Interviewees' Firt and Last Name
  • 5-7 in depth questions
  • Backup Questions
  • Notebook or Voice Recording
  • Interviewees' Contact Information
  • Story to Use Interviews for

  1. Find interviewees who are willing to spend the short duration of time answering your questions, start with their first and last name as you'll need it to quote in your article.

    This allows you to be able to go back to your information and cite the quotes properly.

  2. Filter you interview questions to get the best qualitly of information in the short quantity of time.

    This is necessary to get the most important information in the short amount of time of the interview.

    Make sure to have background knowledge on your interviewee to be sure they are the right fit for giving inforamtion on this topic.

    Have backup questions incase the interviewee brings up differnt points in their answers.

  3. Get as much information in the time given, wirh recording all responses along the way.

    Do not be worried to ask an interviewee to explain something they said, you want to make sure you report about their point of view correctly..

  4. When the interview is completed, thank the interviewee for their time and ask them if they have any questions for you.

    Get their contact information if needed to be able to clarify their responses when you go through them next..
